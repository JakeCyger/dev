Main #1:
Widowmaker.



Widowmaker is a sniper that deals high damage to any character. She is powerful in many ways and can one-shot a dps character winning a team fight or influencing it greatly.


Damage.

Widowmaker, or "Widow", is a DPS sniper so you cna expect her to do ALOT of damage. One thing to understand about Widow is that her sniper charges up to make a shot. At 0% percent charge a shot to the body is 12 damage. 0% to the head is 120 damage. But, when her sniper is fully charged she does 150% more damage. That means that to the body she does 30 damage, to the head it does 300 damage. Now that's ALOT.

Abilities.

Widowmaker has two abilities. Her abilities can be used as transportation, defense, and as a good way to lay a trap. Her first ability is called "Grapple". In this ability, she throws a grapple and hooks onto the nearest ledge. Cooldown is 12 seconds. In her next ability "Venom Mine". Widowmaker

Ults.

The word "Ult" refers to Ultimate. It is the characters strongest move but has the longest time till you can use it. This move can be charged passivly or in combat. Damage differs and some Ults help your own team rather than do damage.
